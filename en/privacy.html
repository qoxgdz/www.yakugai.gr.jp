Privacy Policy

Medwatcher Japan Privacy Statement

We collect two types of personal information: (1) technical information that is automatically collected from all visitors to the Medwatcher Japan site; and (2) information that you voluntarily submit to us.

We shall use personal information within the extent necessary to achieve the following purposes, and shall not use personal information for any other purposes without your consent except as otherwise provided for by laws or ordinances.

  • Tracking the number and frequency of hits per page, in order to design our site more useful and visitor-oriented.
  • Sending and receiving communications relating to the activities of the Medwatcher Japan.
  • Providing information concerning the activities of the Medwatcher Japan, including but not limited to its lectures and seminars.
  • Responding to various inquires.

We shall not provide personal information to any third party without your consent except as otherwise provided for by laws or ordinances.

Please contact the office of Medwatcher Japan (email: yakugai@t3.rim.or.jp) for any comments, questions, requests for disclosure and other inquiries concerning the treatment of personal information.
